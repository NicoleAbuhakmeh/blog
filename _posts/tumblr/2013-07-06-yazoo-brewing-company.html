The beautiful sampler selection from Yazoo Brewing Company in Nashville, Tennessee.

The brewery is located The Gulch section of Nashville. The brewery featured an expanded taproom with 18 taps and a patio for outdoor seating with a forty barrel brewing system.

Linus Hall and his wife Lila are both from Mississippi, and moved to Nashville in 1996. Linus had been home brewing since his college days but after years of perfecting his signature ale styles, testing them out on family and friends, he decided to open his own brewery.

The name Yazoo is taken from the Yazoo River, which runs through Hall’s hometown, Vicksburg, Mississippi.

Great crafts on tap include:

Pale Ale

Dos Perros

Hefeweizen

Hop Project (ongoing series of IPA’s)

Sue

Sly Rye Porter

Hap & Harry’s Lynchburg Lager

They also have a variety of their Seasonal Beers that are usually available on tap.
