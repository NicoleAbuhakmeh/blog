Thirsty Dog Brewing Co. in Akron, OH was definitely a sight for tired road-tripped eyes. Tucked away on the outskirts of downtown Akron in a humble slightly industrial area, is Thirsty Dog’s recently expanded brewery and newly opened taproom.  The only sign that we were in the right place was the mini grain silo with the Thirsty Dog logo on it!

The taproom is in the middle of the brewery which gives you a great sense of beer in every direction.  We were there on a late Friday afternoon just to squeeze in a few samples or a fresh pint, so we didn’t get to experience any real brewer action.  The production and shipping were in full force though.  

No real sampler as of yet can be had in their recently added Taproom, but you can order as many smaller oz glasses as you’d like. On this day I had:

Labrador Lager

Raspberry Ale

Old Druid Bloodhound Saison

Thirsty Dog is a mid-west staple, and slowly making it’s name on the east coast with their very popular Old Leghumper.  If you find yourself in the area, stop on by this up and comer.  Especially if you love dogs.
