I started keeping track of my craft beering in August of 2012 on my BFF Untappd, after becoming infatuated with the indie - craft beer boom.  I wanted to try everything! 

Now, because I love my liver and didn’t want to drive him mad - I set out with a different goal than trying over 20,000+ brews.  I set my mind to visiting a brewery in each of the 50 states by the age of 50.  While I have a few years to go on that goal, I felt I needed a smaller goal for the next 2 years. 

The goal is to drink a beer from each state.  

I set this goal without doing research on the backwards laws of several states. (I’m looking at you Alabama.) 

Being the data nerd that I am, I knew I would need to some how get my data from untappd to a map. I set about in the old fashion way of going page by page of my history.  That’s when my amazing boyfriend Khalid, told me he could build me a little something to make my life easier. (Read more about that here!)    

I had done some traveling and tried to manually keep track of the beers states I was knocking off, but it’s not that easy some times - especially after a few sampling flights. 

Fast forward 9 months of paper tracking and trying to remember if I had this state or not when I was bootlegging beer back home.  The gods at Untappd must have heard my pleas to add an excel column to the export with the brewery’s address/state - because now as a supporter you have the ability to export that exact information! Greg - You’re awesome. 

I am almost there. 9 more states to go!  These last few are proving to be tricky. Rhode Island should have been crossed off on my New England Beercation but we somehow missed that state, I know we travelled through it to get to Cape Cod. Peter Griffin, I’ll be back to drink your beer! 

But the last few remaining are Rhode Island, Arizona, Alabama, Arkansas, Iowa, Kansas, New Mexico, South and North Dakota. 

The map at the top shows all that is left to complete on my mission. 

Just a few left. Com’on Mr. Liver - get in the car - we have some states to visit!
