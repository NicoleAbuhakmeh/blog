A nice highlight of my June trip to Cleveland was Fat Head’s Saloon.  You’ve seen their logo around - Fat faced, big headed bald guy with mustache, sunglasses and depending on the season or style of beer he might be wearing a santa hat or a german alpine hat. 

This beer is widely loved in the Great Lakes - Western Pennsylvania area. I’d heard about it, but never really experienced it until heading to their saloon. I’m glad I waited until getting to the mothership to taste their craft brews. 

The set up isn’t all that original for the restaurant and typical brew-pub fair, but the sampler was really grand. 

My beer list for my trip to the saloon included: 

  • Oompa Loompa Chocolate Cream Stout
  • Tibetan Tribal Tripel
  • Google Fogger Hefe Weizen
  • Nuke Proof Wheat Wine
  • Bumbleberry Honey Blueberry Ale
  • Starlight Lager
  • Duke of Lager Fest Bier
  • Head Hunter IPA
