Bad Wolf Brewing Company was our last stop on the first day of our NoVa/Central Virginia Beercation.  

I wasn’t quite sure if I was going to be able to make it inside the small retail front shop to taste Bad Wolf’s unfiltered and unpasteurized beers.  The small space they have as a taproom was packed!  This was a good sign, even if it made it difficult to get into the space and order a sampler.  

Bad Wolf’s set up is that of a one barrel brewery. It’s nice because that means there is always something new on tap. Their six taps on this day included: 

  • Loveless Brown Porter
  • Malupa Milk Stout
  • Citra Saison
  • Citra Gold
  • Ruinator IPA
  • Bernis Imperial Stout

Someday I will learn that I don’t need to try every single beer a brewery has on tap. Some day.
