This quaint small scale microbrewery in the town of York, Pennsylvania has heart! Their website touts that they are devoted to bringing the flavor back to beer. They use a custom built, electric-fired 4.5 bbl brewing system with a combination mash/lauter tun.

The Name… Liquid Hero was technically created by all three of the brewers.  Upon their night of brewing beer, a sentence came out that I’m sure all of us beer lovers have uttered at one point or another. “Beer is my liquid hero.”  

They have a beautiful set up with a great sampler collection – every beer on tap is worth a try once, find your favorite and order a pint the next time! Don’t forget to take home a growler too!
