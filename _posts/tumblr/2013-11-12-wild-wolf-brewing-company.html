Wild Wolf Brewing Company, not to be confused with my last brewery post - Bad Wolf, is farther south and another one of the great stops along the Nelson 151 Brew Trail in Nellysford, Virginia. 

We pulled into Wild Wolf’s mini Brewery-Restaurant-Shopping oasis for a nightcap- and in true ConsumptionYeasta style - that would be a sampler of their finest draft offerings! 

Our sampler on this day included: 

  • Double IPA
  • Howling Pumpkin
  • White Wolf
  • The Local Wolf
  • Alpha Ale
  • Dry Stout
  • Wee Heavy
  • Blonde Hunny
  • American Pilsner

Wild Wolf has a little bit of something for everyone.  If you don’t find something you like to drink on tap, there is a homebrew store on site also! 

But remember folks…
