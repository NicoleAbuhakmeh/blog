Roy Pitz Brewing Company in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania has one of the best tasting Watermelon Lager’s on the East Coast. Lovitz Watermelon Lager is the standout in this sampler. Other great beers sampled at the Beer Stube include:

  • Ludwig’s Revenge
  • Old Jail Ale
  • Laydown Stay Down
  • Falling Spring Lager
  • Downtown Abbey Brown
  • Gobbler Lager
  • Best Blonde
  • Daddy Fat Sacks

Roy Pitz isn’t just beer, It’s Liquid Art! Each beer has a great label created to represent it. 

Visit them at the beer stube/brewery:
