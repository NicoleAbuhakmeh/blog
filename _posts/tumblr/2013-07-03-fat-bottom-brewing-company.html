Fat Bottom Brewing Company in East Nashville - (East Nasty or Nashvegas to the locals) has one of the best layouts for any brewery around. Also the friendliest staff.

Fat Bottom’s full flavored brews go with their pin-up style!

Their craft line up includes:

Ginger Wheat Ale

Ruby Red American Ale

Black Betty IPA

Bertha Oatmeal Stout (who knew something called Bertha could be so sexy!)

and Java Jane Coffee Porter

 

Visit them at: 

900 Main Street

Nashville, TN 37206
