Devils Backbone Brewing Company Basecamp Brewpub in Roseland, VA was our 1st stop along the Nelson 151 Brewery Trail. They have a grand resort style “basecamp” restaurant and brewery set up. From the parking lot area I could see they had a stage area beginning to be built for outdoor events.

The real reason we were there was to give their 2012 Great American Beer Festival winning beers a try. 

Tonight’s sampler included: 

Schwartz Bier

Striped Bass Pale Ale

Black Rock Milk Stout

White Stag

Hazy Summer

Old Virginia Gold

My favorite was the Schwartz Bier, it paired really well with my delicious pulled pork sandwich. 

Next time, I’ve gotta get there early enough to get the chocolate crunch torte before they sell out.
