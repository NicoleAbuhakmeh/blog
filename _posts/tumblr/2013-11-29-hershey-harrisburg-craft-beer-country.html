I often forget how close I live to great beer.  I can travel less than a mile to get to a local Appalachian Brewing Company location, yet haven’t lately. I could drive an extra 5 minutes to get to Troegs but, I haven’t been there in over a year. I have no excuse really. I guess you could say that I just take it for granted. “I’ll get back to Troegs when (Insert cousin, blah blah) gets into town for college break.” Or some other lame reason.

I travel/Beercation to try new craft beer. It’s been my thing for a while now. When I get back, I don’t get the usual “How was your trip?” question, I get “What was your favorite beer you had?” My friends and family know me too well. But on my recent New England Beercation in September, I filling out my Maine Beer Trail Passport, and I kept thinking - I WANT TO BRING THIS AWESOMENESS TO PA!  I want to get people to travel to my area to try the sours from Pizza Boy, or the sweet inventions from The Brewery at Hershey. Well, I guess someone beat me to the punch, Meet - "Hershey Harrisburg Craft Beer Country“ 

Breweries include: 

  • Als of Hampden/Pizza Boy Brewing Co.
  • Alter Ego Brewing Company
  • Appalachian Brewing Company
  • Bube’s Brewery
  • JoBoy’s Brew Pub
  • Lancaster Brewing Company
  • Market Cross Pub & Brewery
  • Millbock Brewing Company
  • Saint Boniface Craft Brewing Company
  • Snitz Creek Brewery
  • The Brewery at Hershey
  • Troegs Brewing Company

We may not give out the cool goodies like they do on those other brew passports, but Vermont/Maine are known as a mecca for craft beer. We here in Pennsylvania have to break out of the "Oh, yah, yous guys have that ‘thar lager. What’s it called again? Oh Yuengslings.” (Yes, actual conversation from my trip to Michigan) Now, don’t think i’m Yuengling hatin’, it’s usually my go to session beer, but it’s not new to me. I’d like to see Pennsylvania’s little cutout part of that Craft Beer Map of the US become something other than Yuengling though. WE ARE… not just Yuengling. (Or Penn State, but that’s for another post!)

I think this is a creative way to break out of that! Someone may have beat me to the punch with this, but i’m cool with that because it actually exists! Woo-Hoo, You Go Pennsylvania!
