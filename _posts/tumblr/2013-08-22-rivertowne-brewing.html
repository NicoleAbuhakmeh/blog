“If you’re going to Pittsburgh, you have to try this Pineapple Beer from Rivertowne.”  Now like I said in my Pittsburgh Beercation post, Up until last year I wasn’t fond of fruit in my beer or even fruit flavored beer.  I had my reservations for sure, but I’m also not one to turn down trying any beer i’ve never had before. 

Rivertowne has several locations in and around Pittsburgh. Since we were going for food and beer we decided to give the Monroeville Rivertowne Pour House a go. If you go, order a cheese & steak stromboli to go with your own sampler. 

Most of the locations carry between 14-18 beers. I made my own sampler of 8 of their best. 

  • Red, Red Raspberry Ale
  • Hefeweizen
  • Old Wylie’s IPA
  • Grateful White
  • Hala Kahiki Pineapple Ale
  • RT Lager
  • Maxwell’s Scottish Ale
  • Babbling Blonde

I need to return to get some of their seasonal brews. I love a good Milk Stout afterall!
