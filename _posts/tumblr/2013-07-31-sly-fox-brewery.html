This past weekend I made it a mission to visit 2 of the breweries I’ve been missing out on located in the outskirts of Philadelphia. Thunderstorms nor flash flooding was going to stop me on this mission.

Sly Fox was the first on the list. I love taking roadtrips. But when Beer is involved it becomes a Beercation - no matter the length of time spent away from home. This beercation involved visiting breweries in Delaware and Pennsylvania.  

Sly Fox is tucked away in a little strip mall in Phoenixville. Since it is widely distributed in my area, I’ve had a few of their cans, even a few of the topless 360 cans of Helles Golden Lager.  This was my first sampling of a few of their main stream brews though. 

They serve their samplers on a neat little tray. I love a brewery that knows how to serve a sampler. 

They had about 15 beers on tap, but with my previous experience with Sly Fox, I tried to get things out of my reach!

My sampler on this day included:

  • Wegman’s Summer IPA
  • Grisette
  • Saison Vos
  • O'Reilly’s Stout
  • Phoenix Pale Ale
  • Chester County Brown

I had a great first visit to Sly Fox, next time I’ll leave room for some items off their menu.

Visit them!
