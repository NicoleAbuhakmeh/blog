The final stop along our Nelson 151 Brew trail was for brunch at Blue Mountain Brewery located towards the entrance/end of the Skyline Drive.  Fueling up for our drive home and since I wasn’t driving (Thanks babe!) I added the full line up sampler to sip while waiting on our brunch order. Today’s sampler included: 

  • Collaboration Rye Barchetta
  • Blue Reserve
  • Dark Hollow
  • Full Nelson
  • Rockfish Wheat
  • Kölsch 151
  • Classic Lager
  • Local Species
  • Über Pils
  • Virginia Draft - this one is made by Bold Rock Hard Cider 

Beautiful line up for a pretty perfect end to another gorgeous trip in Virginia.  As they say…
